Test Series

Our Structured Approach

30% Mock Test Series

Focuses on the most important 30% of the syllabus, selected using Institute priorities and past exam trends. Sets your timetable and direction from day one.

  • Cover & retain the key 30% with writing practice and time management.
  • Identify mistakes early with chapter-wise performance analysis.
  • Build confidence by targeting a smaller, high-value portion first.

60% Mock Test Series

Builds on the first step by adding the next 30% of the syllabus-medium-priority chapters as per past weightage-so you cover ~90% of important concepts.

  • Stronger grasp with broader yet curated coverage.
  • Clarity on medium-priority topics that commonly appear.
  • Momentum to enter the full-syllabus phase with confidence.

100% Mock Test Series (A)

Full-syllabus rehearsal for each subject that tests knowledge depth, writing skills, and time management-your final checkpoint before the real exam.

  • Complete syllabus coverage in a controlled, exam-like setting.
  • Evaluate readiness, accuracy, and speed together.
  • Lock your strategy before the final attempt.

100% Mock Test Series (B)

Treated exactly like the Institute exam-real conditions, real pressure. Spot last-minute mistakes and tighten execution before the actual attempt.

  • Authentic exam experience end-to-end.
  • Objective feedback on final gaps.
  • Confidence boost for the main exam day.
