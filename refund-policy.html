Refund Eligibility Conditions

  1. The offer is valid only if a student attempts and writes at least 1 Group & 3 Tests per subject.
  2. The term "clear" refers to securing 50 marks (i.e., 50%).
  3. A student must score a minimum of 50 marks in each PLEDGECA test paper.
  4. If, in even a single test, the student scores below 50 marks, he/she will not qualify for a refund.
  5. In case a student has enrolled for One Group or Both Groups, he/she must clear all the assigned tests in all subjects. Failing to do so will make the student ineligible for the refund.
  6. The login credentials of PLEDGECA must be shared to verify the ICAI/ICSI/ICMAI results. Screenshots will not be accepted.
  7. Students must also request their certified copies from ICAI/ICSI/ICMAI and share them with us for the refund process to begin.
  8. PLEDGECA assures that all answer sheets received from ICAI/ICSI/ICMAI will be kept strictly confidential.
