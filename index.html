Does PledgeCA offer a free counseling session?

Yes, every student gets a 30+ minute free counseling session with experienced CA, CS & CMA professionals to understand their current level, outline priorities, and finalize a personalized study plan.

Which exams does PledgeCA support?

PledgeCA supports CA (Chartered Accountant) Foundation, Intermediate, and Final levels; CS (Company Secretary) Executive and Professional levels; and CMA (Cost & Management Accountant) Foundation, Intermediate, and Final levels.

What is PledgeCA's Refund Guarantee?

Under our Assurance Policy, if you prepare using ABC analysis, consistently attempt tests, and clear them, you are expected to clear the exams. However, if you are unable to clear despite following the complete process, you will be eligible for a refund as per the policy terms.

How accurate is PledgeCA's test series compared to actual CA exams?

PledgeCA's test series consistently features questions that match 30-50% of questions in the actual CA, CS, and CMA main examinations, designed and evaluated by field experts aligned with ICAI, ICSI, and ICMAI guidelines.
